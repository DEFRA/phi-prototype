Use this service to find information about plant health controls and the pest or disease risks of importing a plant to Great Britain (England, Scotland and Wales).​

  • From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Animal and Plant Health Agency
  • Published 14 September 2023
  • Last updated 23 October 2023 — See all updates

Applies to England and Wales

Use this service to check:​

  • what quarantine pests and diseases a plant could be a host to
  • the restrictions on the imports of some plants​
  • what documentation and plant health controls need to be followed when importing a plant​

You can also:​

  • find current information on non-native plant pests and diseases that are considered a risk to the UK.​

Start now

Before you start

You'll need the name of the plant, pest or disease to find plant health information.

You can use:

  • the scientific name
  • a common name in some cases​

If you need import information about a particular plant, you'll also need the country of origin.

This service does not provide trade information relating to exports.

This service does not have information about pests and diseases native to the UK.

The online service is also available in Welsh (ar gael yn Gymraeg).
