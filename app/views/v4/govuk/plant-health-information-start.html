Guidance for Northern Ireland

Use this service to search for a plant and:​

  • find out which quarantine pests and diseases a plant could carry
  • access the latest rules for importing plants

You can also:​

  • find current information on non-native plant pests and diseases that are considered a risk to the UK.​

Start now

Before you start

You'll need the name of the plant, pest or disease to find plant health information.

You can use:

  • the scientific name
  • a common name in some cases​

This service:

  • does not provide trade information about exporting plants
  • is not a pest or disease identification service​
  • does not have information about pests and diseases native to the UK

The online service is also available in Welsh (ar gael yn Gymraeg).
